Get to know more about us...

Tutoring in New Jersey, We are sometimes asked whether Ivy Tutoring Services offers a “program.” No, we do not. In fact, we believe that one-size-fits-all instructional programs can often be part of the problem, rather than the solution.
The one-size-fits-all instructional programs that are used by some tutoring services and learning centers are often rigid without consideration for the student’s actual challenges and needs, and can lead to further frustration. With these programs, the tutoring may not relate at all to what your child is working on in school. We believe tutoring should take a different approach and be focused on mastering the student’s school curriculum, based on their particular challenges and needs.
Through one-on-one interaction, our tutors can determine a student’s strengths and weaknesses and create an individualized approach to address any problem areas. This approach is flexible and allows the tutor and student to creatively work through challenging subject matters. With the help of our Director we match the best tutors with the students.

Our tutors
